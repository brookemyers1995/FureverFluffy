Schedule

Please note that the groomer has a very busy schedule and time slots fill up fast! The salon is typically booked out a few weeks in advance. This ensures that your pet has a peaceful experience and receives the best quality. To book an appointment,simply call/text the phone number provided on the site or fill out a contact form located on the contact us section. Thank you for your understanding and we look forward to meeting you and your furry friend(s)!
