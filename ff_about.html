About Furever Fluffy

Furever Fluffy was founded by Brooke Myers, a passionate animal lover with a vision to create a grooming salon that feels like a second home for pets. Considering Brooke has an extensive past working in professional grooming, She decided to pursue a plan to establish a dog grooming facility that is not only for service, but also an experience for both the owner and their furry friend. Brooke started off in a small, single-room operation, and soon Furever Fluffy has grown into a full-service grooming salon, thanks to our loyal customers and their word-of-mouth recommendations. Over the years, we’ve expanded our services to include specialized grooming for all breeds, skin and coat treatments, and even spa packages to indulge your pets. Our team of professional groomers is the heart and soul of Furever Fluffy. Every member of the team has a talented skill that they bare and all have the certification and experience needed for the job.
Brooke, the founder of Furever Fluffy, has over 8 years of experience in the pet grooming industry and specializes in breed-specific cuts and creative grooming. Jessica, Furever Fluffy's lead groomer from Austin, Texas, has over 10 years of experience and specializes in breed cuts. Jessica is also known to have a gentle touch and soothing voice that the dogs loves. Maya, a certified pet stylist, has been with Furever Fluffy since 2020 and is renowned for her meticulous attention to detail and artistic flair.


Conveniently located in the heart of Daytona Beach, Furever Fluffy is easily accessible for all your pet grooming needs. We invite you to visit us and experience the Furever Fluffy difference. Whether your pet needs a quick bath, a stylish haircut, or a luxurious spa day, our dedicated team is here to provide exceptional care and service.

Thank you for choosing Furever Fluffy. We look forward to making your pet a part of our family!
