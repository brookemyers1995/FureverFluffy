About Furever Fluffy

Welcome to Furever Fluffy, where your pet’s comfort and happiness are our top priorities. My name is Brooke, and I’m the heart and hands behind this home-based grooming salon in Deltona, Florida.

With a decade of experience working with dogs and 5 years as a professional groomer, I’ve developed a deep love and understanding of our furry friends. My journey in pet care is driven by a passion for animals and a commitment to providing the highest grooming services in a relaxed, stress-free environment.

In addition to running Furever Fluffy, I’m a part-time college student and a single mom, balancing the joys and challenges of both school and parenthood. On weekends, I also work at a salon, further honing my skills and keeping up with the latest trends in grooming and care.

At Furever Fluffy, I offer a range of services tailored to your dog’s unique needs, from breed-specific cuts to creative styles, always with a gentle touch and attention to detail. I’m committed to using only the best products to ensure your pet’s skin and coat stay healthy and beautiful.

Every dog that walks through our doors becomes a part of the Furever Fluffy family. I’m dedicated to making sure your pet not only looks great but feels great too. Thank you for trusting me with your furry friend—I can’t wait to meet them!
