About Furever Fluffy

Furever Fluffy was founded by Brooke Myers, a passionate animal lover with a vision to create a grooming salon that feels like a second home for pets. With a background in professional grooming and years of experience in pet care, Brooke set out to build a place where grooming is not just a service, but an experience that both pets and their owners look forward to. Starting as a small, single-room operation, Furever Fluffy has grown into a full-service grooming salon, thanks to our loyal customers and their word-of-mouth recommendations. Over the years, we’ve expanded our services to include specialized grooming for all breeds, skin and coat treatments, and even spa packages to indulge your pets. Our team of professional groomers is the heart and soul of Furever Fluffy. Each member brings unique skills and a genuine love for animals, ensuring that your pets receive the best care possible.

Brooke, the founder of Furever Fluffy, has over 8 years of experience in the pet grooming industry and specializes in breed-specific cuts and creative grooming. Jessica, our lead groomer from Austin, Texas, has over 10 years of experience and is known for her gentle touch and exceptional skill with scissors. Maya, a certified pet stylist, has been with Furever Fluffy since 2020 and is renowned for her meticulous attention to detail and artistic flair.

Conveniently located in the heart of Daytona Beach, Furever Fluffy is easily accessible for all your pet grooming needs. We invite you to visit us and experience the Furever Fluffy difference. Whether your pet needs a quick bath, a stylish haircut, or a luxurious spa day, our dedicated team is here to provide exceptional care and service.

Thank you for choosing Furever Fluffy. We look forward to making your pet a part of our family!
