About Furever Fluffy

Furever Fluffy was founded by Brooke Myers, a professional dog groomer who has a deep affinity for animals. Brooke had a vision to create a grooming salon that is not only a place to perform grooming services on dogs, but to also be a home away from home. Brooke started off grooming dogs on the side which quickly grew into a mobile grooming service. In a short period of time, she was able to put her dream into action, thus the birth of Furever Fluffy dog grooming salon, located in the heart of Daytona Beach, Florida. Since 2019, Furver Fluffy has been booming with business thanks to the word of mouth from our loyal regulars! We pride ourselves on the capabilities of our well-trained team. Each groomer has a niche they excel in and love to express their creativity through their grooming abilities.

Brooke, the founder of Furever Fluffy, has over 8 years of experience in the pet grooming industry and has a knack for creative grooming. Jessica, our lead groomer from Brooklyn, New York, has over 10 years of experience and specializes in breed cuts. Maya, a certified pet stylist, has been with Furever Fluffy since 2020 and is well-known for her artistic ability.

Whether your pet needs a quick bath, a stylish haircut, or a luxurious spa day, the Furever Fluffy team is eager to accommodate you and your canine child’s needs.

Thank you for choosing Furever Fluffy. We look forward to making you and your dog a part of our family!
